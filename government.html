Government Form of the Gaelic Kingdom of Mabruenia

The Gaelic Kingdom of Mabruenia is a Constitutional Monarchy that was founded September 29th, 2024 via a vote with the citizens of Mabruenia. The Kingdom is ran by the House of Fleming. The government outside of the Monarchy consists of the Executive Branch, which include the Chancellor, Deputy Chancellor, and seven Ministries. The Kingdom also has the Legislative and Judicial Branches, where the Legislative Branch includes two houses, being the House of Lords as the Upper House, and House of Representatives as the lower House. The HoL is a non-elected branch, consisting of experts and appointed nobles in the kingdom, while the HoR is an elected branch, consisting of officials voted in from each province. There are three provinces within Mabruenia, named Val Regalis, Civtas Streda, and Oria Montes. Each Province is ruled by a governor. The Judicial Branch of Mabruenia consists of the Supreme Court of Mabruenia, and other inferior courts, used in tangem with the Ministry of Justice.
